HISTORIC MARKETPLACE

Project Architect: Ligia Saldaña Martorell, AIA, CAAPPR

Design Team: Atelier 66, csp

Location: Ponce, Puerto Rico

Year: 2006

Historic Marketplace

This project is located in Ponce’s historic district. It was originally built in the 1860’s and has gone through several adaptations throughout the years. In the late 1990’s it was converted into a small shopping center and later rented out to a department store. Having been in disuse for more than five years the municipality decided to restore it to its original use. The design solution pays attention to the traditional construction techniques while adding modern elements.
