SCHIZZO PROJECTS

URBAN INFILL

Project Architect: Ligia Saldaña Martorell, AIA, CAAPPR

Design Team: Atelier 66, csp

Location: Guayama, Puerto Rico

Year: 2004

Guayama Historic Center Urban Infill

This project is consists of fifteen-(15) new structures disperse throughout the town’s historic district. All proposed projects were unique and responded to the towns scale and surroundings. Four-(4) structures were institutional in nature while eleven other interventions were destined for residential use. The complexity of the design solution was mainly in responding to the sensitive urban environment and historic precedents. The construction phase was carried out by one single general contractor who, together with our supervision effort; had the challenge of phasing the projects.
