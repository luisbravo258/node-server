UPR Ponce

Project Architect: Ligia Saldaña Martorell, AIA, CAAPPR

Design Team: Atelier 66, csp

Location: Ponce, Puerto Rico

Year: 2008

UPR- Adelina Coppin Alvarado Library- Ponce Campus

This project is a restoration effort. The existing 1970’s building had gone through a lot of alterations rendering all existing spaces and infrastructure inefficient. The administration was convinced, after presented with our full analysis and proposed new layout; to update all aspects of the facilities. The once not used library is now a hub of information were students meet to work and socialize as well.
