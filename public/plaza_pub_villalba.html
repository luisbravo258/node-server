PLAZA PUB

Project Architect: Ligia Saldaña Martorell, AIA, CAAPPR

Design Team: Atelier 66, csp

Location: Villalba, Puerto Rico

Year: 2011

Plaza Pub

This project is located in town’s center. It is owned by the municipality and provides a small marketplace venue for local artisans. It is modern in flair but acknowledges the scale and spatial realities of a small town in the center of the island. The proposal uses cross ventilation and natural lighting thus keeping cost down for the client.
