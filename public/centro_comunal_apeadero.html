Apeadero- Community Center

This project is located in the mountains of Villalba, PR; in a small agricultural community. The community itself is much dispersed and this facility provides a needed meeting point for all the generations that call Apeadero home. The facility is integrated to its surroundings and to the beautiful views. Is an open plan that seems to continually reach out to nature at every turn.
