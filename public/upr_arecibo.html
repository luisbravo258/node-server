UPR Arecibo

Project Architect: Ligia Saldaña Martorell, AIA, CAAPPR

Design Team: SCHIZZO

Location: Arecibo, Puerto Rico

Years: 2015-2016

UPR- New Learning Commons Building - Arecibo Campus

This project is in its final design phase and scheduled for construction in early 2016. It will become a beacon within the campus community. It will bring the student community into the 21st century since it will house all technological tools necessary for group learning, access to research data bases and a cyber café included as well.
