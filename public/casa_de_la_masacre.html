CASA DE LA MASACRE

Project Architect: Ligia Saldaña Martorell, AIA, CAAPPR

Design Team: Atelier 66, csp

Location: Ponce, Puerto Rico

Year: 2013

Casa de la Masacre Museum

This project consists of restoration work. The structure dates back to the early 20th century and was the venue for a historical political incident which occurred in the 1930’s. The structured had been restored previously but a general upgrading was required. All historical construction techniques were used for the restoration work.
