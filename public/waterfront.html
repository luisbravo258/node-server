Waterfront

Project Architect: Ligia Saldaña Martorell, AIA, CAAPPR

Design Team: SCHIZZO

Location: Ponce, Puerto Rico

Years: 2014-2016

Remodeling of Santa Isabel’s Waterfront Facilities

This project is awaiting construction. The existing waterfront facilities have been experiencing great disrepair compromising the integrity of its structural system. Our proposal address this situation and provides a new structure which almost doubles the existing usable area while being sensible to its location on the water. The design focuses on materiality and the integration of elements that celebrate the view.
